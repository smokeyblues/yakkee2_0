What is Yakkee?

Yakkee is designed to bring friends and loved ones together and provide opportunity for interactivity like games and shared experiences without having to physically visit one another.

Some types of interactivity include games like chess, checkers and Go Fish. You can also watch movies and shows together while on video chat. So you can make inferences and wise cracks about what you are watching in real time.
